# Internal Communications

## Communication Types

Load the appropriate guideline from `examples/`:
- `3p-updates.md` - Progress/Plans/Problems team updates
- `company-newsletter.md` - Company-wide newsletters
- `faq-answers.md` - Frequently asked questions
- `general-comms.md` - Other internal communications

## 3P Update Format

### Progress
- What was accomplished this period
- Metrics and milestones achieved
- Blockers resolved

### Plans
- What will be done next period
- Key priorities and focus areas
- Dependencies and timeline

### Problems
- Current blockers and challenges
- Risks and concerns
- Help needed

## Guidelines

- Keep concise and scannable
- Use bullet points for easy reading
- Highlight key metrics
- Be specific about timelines
- Call out blockers clearly
